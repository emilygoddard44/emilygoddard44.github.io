Site Plan: Temple Inn & Suites

Emily Goddard

Overview

Purpose

To build a website for the Temple Inn & Suite hotel chain that will provide future users with material about specialized services, accommodations, contact information, and reservations.

Audience

Members of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints who need to find services such as accommodations, changing rooms, babysitting, and reception areas during their temple visit.

Typography

Heading Font: Roboto Condensed

A less formal font to create a relaxed, homey feel without being too casual.

Paragraph Font: Lato

A font for variety while maintaining the overall goal of creating a comfortable environment.

Normal paragraph example

We offer a complete package for whatever your needs are in relation to temple service and accomodations. Our shuttle runs to meet the endowment schedule and our babysitting servcies provide an afforable and safe alternative for your family.

Colored paragraph example

Plan on using our free changing room for you and your fellow patrons as you transition from traveling to the temple to actually entering the temple. We want you to feel comfortable changing and freshing up in order to focus on meaningful service. Let us take care of you.
